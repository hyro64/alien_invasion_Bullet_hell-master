In Alien invasion, A top down bullet hell shooter. The player controls a ship that appears at the bottom center of the screen. The player can move up the ship up and down and shoot bullets using the spacebar. when the game begins, a fleet of aliens fills the sky and moves across and down the screen. The player shoots and detros the aliens. If the player shoots all the aliens, a new fleet appears tha move faster then the prevoius fleet. If any alien hits the player's ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the players loses a ship. If the player losse three ships, the games ends.
